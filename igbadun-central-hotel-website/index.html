About us

Igbadun Central is a luxury hotel created to meet the special need of customers, who are desirous of having a home away from experience. From the spacious rooms, you can marvel at the amazing view of the city its floor-to-ceiling windows. Igbadun Central Hotel offers exquisite facilities like 3 tennis courts, volleyball courts, 3 squash courts and a basketball court, anoutdoor pool, fitness centre, business centre with internet access,suites and six restaurants. These restaurants offer a variety of Mediterranean and Nigerian delicacies. Properly secured and just few mins from the airport.
